Who is Karel?

Simple, free and very obedient robot.
He knows only a few commands, but you can make honest results while using them. It is a simplified variant of two significant programming languages: C++ and Java.
No jokes, programming is easier then you think. Obtain Karel and ensure it.

Learn. Grow up. Help others.

Our main goals is to help people to grow up!
... and certainly make this world better! Karel will help you to learn the basic principles of programming.
Train your abstract mind in simple and joyful way.

Why should I try?

SHPP Karel is the best Karel The Robot fork ever made!
Looking for a easy way to get involved in programming? Prefage game-based studing? Rather you need Karel. Wann'a an easy manual page, comfortable web interface and 3D graphics? Then press the Big Red Button and go coding!
